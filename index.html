A New York University Based Literary Magazine.

For over a decade, Aftab has built itself into a voice for minorities and a platform for self discovery & stories of identity. Born in the Islamic Center at NYU, Aftab serves as the outlet for cultural, spiritual, and self expressive pieces whether they be poetry, prose, visual arts, or photography. No matter who you are, we invite you to take part in our narrative by submitting something of your own.
